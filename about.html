About NyayaAI

NyayaAI is an AI‑powered legal companion built to make legal information accessible to every Indian citizen. We break down complex laws into simple, actionable guidance — no jargon, no fees, no barriers.

Our platform uses a curated knowledge base of Indian legal principles and an AI assistant to help you understand your rights, draft complaints, and navigate everyday legal challenges. We do not replace lawyers, but we empower you to take the first step with confidence.
